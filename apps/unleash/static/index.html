Live CBBA auction state. Each card is one announced task. Every robot computes its own bid score locally — score = dot(my_capability, required_capability) · urgency − α·distance − β·risk — and publishes bids over gossip. The winner is the robot whose bid is currently the highest; winners propagate on the unleash/task_winner topic. A task shows assigned once any bid has been recorded, and its winner may still change as better bids arrive (or if the current winner is killed / blacklisted).
